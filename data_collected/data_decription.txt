csv column names:
type, millis, sensor_x, sensor_y, sensor_z


type:
10 ------- TYPE_LINEAR_ACCELERATION
4 ------- TYPE_GYROSCOPE

millis:
the number of milliseconds between the current computer time and GMT (Greenwich mean time) at 0:00:00 on January 1, 1970

sensor_x, sensor_y, sensor_z:
raw data collected by android device.

For more details about type and sensor values, please refer to https://developer.android.com/guide/topics/sensors/sensors_motion?hl=zh-cn
